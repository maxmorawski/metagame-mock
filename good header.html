Frequently asked questions

When and where is Metagame 2026?
Metagame runs November 6–8, 2026 at Lighthaven in Berkeley, California — one of the Bay Area's most distinctive event venues. Doors open Friday afternoon and programming runs through Sunday evening.
What's included with my ticket?
Your badge gets you all three days of talks, collaborative challenges, tournaments, the game library, and the Friday Night Market. Some special events with limited capacity use free sign-ups announced in the program.
Do I need to be a hardcore gamer to enjoy it?
Not at all. Attendees range from professional game designers and puzzle constructors to people who just love playing. If you're curious about games, strategy, or puzzles, you'll find your table.
Can I run my own game or event?
Yes — Metagame is built by and for its attendees, and community-run events are the heart of the weekend. We also offer a Patron track for people who want to fund and run something more ambitious, like a megagame or puzzle hunt. See the sponsorship page or reach out.
Is there an age requirement?
Attendees under 18 are welcome with a ticketed parent or guardian. Some evening events may be marked 18+ in the program.
What food options are available?
Light snacks and drinks are available at the venue throughout the weekend, and Lighthaven is surrounded by Berkeley restaurants within easy walking distance. Meal breaks are built into the schedule.
What's the refund policy?
Tickets are fully refundable until 30 days before the event, and transferable to another person any time before doors open. Email us and we'll sort it out.
Is the venue accessible?
We work with Lighthaven to keep main spaces and programming accessible. If you have specific access needs, tell us ahead of time at team@metagame.games and we'll make it work.

Can't find what you're looking for? Email team@metagame.games and a human will get back to you.
