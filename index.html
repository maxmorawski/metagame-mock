The convention

I Once Again Don My Hat and Mask

Metagame started as one woman's manic dream. Now that dream has been passed to me. It burns hot in my skull.

So come, ye, to my con. Come and rejoice, for the games are upon us once more. Solve my puzzles. Hear the secrets. If you are bold of heart, venture to purchase a ticket; if you are curious but craven, subscribe to the newsletter. Perhaps you will learn more. Perhaps it will matter.
