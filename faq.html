Frequently asked questions

For real, what is this convention?
To be frank with you, it is hard to describe. Metagame has many of the typical trappings of a board game convention: gaming spaces, designers talking about their projects, as well games requiring more organization, such as Blood on the Clocktower. But it's a little weirder, too. Last year, the entire con was one large game. Players discovered their team by solving a puzzle on their swag, and spent three days competing against one another while still playing the other games.

Beyond that, even weirder stuff happens. Last year someone came and built an entire escape room, which ended up being many people's highlight. Another guest started a secret, second convention-wide game that staff only learned of in the closing hours. At one point, there were people knife-fighting with tasers. We hope to have even more of this sort of thing in 2026, so come by and see what happens.

Will I be nourished?
The grounds will be littered with beverages and snacks. They are yours for the taking. Trucks, laden with food, will arrive, from time to time, and you may purchase as you will.
